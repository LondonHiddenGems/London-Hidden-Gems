THE POSTAL MUSEUM

It was opened in 2004 as the British Postal Museum & archive by the Postal Heritage Trust. It will take you through the history of postal services, revealing really interesting fun facts about our mail.

If you visit it, you will also have the chance to ride through the hidden tunnels or walk through the 100-year-old mail rail tunnels. So, do not miss this one.
